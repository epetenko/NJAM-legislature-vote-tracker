NJ Cannabis Insider periodically will poll the 120 members of the Legislature to ask where they stand on legalization. Most lawmakers say they are undecided or declined to respond. Some won't weigh in until they see the bill; others privately say they will lose bargaining power if they tip their hand too early. This will be updated as we move forward.
